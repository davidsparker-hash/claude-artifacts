Immediate ask total$66,000Bridge only: operating plan funded by seed
Recommended path — hybrid

A short-term convertible bridge note funds the first 90 days (Roger + legal/setup, ~$66K), then converts into the priced seed at the round close. This protects the start date without locking full round terms early. Cap, discount, and conversion trigger to be finalized with counsel.

Bridge scope

The bridge funds only Roger's 3-month engagement and legal/corporate setup (~$66K) during the 90-day window. It does not fund the 18-month operating plan, team buildout, or infrastructure — those are funded by the seed itself.
