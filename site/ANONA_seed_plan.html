Total investor stake at exit (base) is $8,756,127 on $3.75M invested. Recommendation

The data supports a $15M–$22M post-money. The model runs the base ~$19M — the market-standard ~20% dilution and AI-seed median. The tension: a higher valuation means less dilution for the founder but a lower multiple for investors. At ~$19M the base case returns ~2.3×; holding 3×+ at that valuation leans on the upside exit. Switch the post-money input to stress-test the $15M conservative or $22M aggressive case — every return re-prices automatically.
