How to read it

The base case returns ~2.3× at a defensible valuation; 3×+ comes from the upside — a higher exit multiple or ARR above ~$6.9M. Valuation is triangulated by two independent methods (comparable seeds and a Series-A back-check), both landing near $19M.

Illustrative; reconciles to the ANONA seed model. Exit stake assumes ~55% ownership retained through future dilution and a base 15× exit multiple. Figures and terms (including bridge cap / discount) to be finalized with counsel. Not investment or legal advice. · May 2026.
